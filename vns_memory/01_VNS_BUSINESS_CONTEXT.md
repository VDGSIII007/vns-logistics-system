# VNS Business Context

## Company

VNS Logistics Services Phils. Corp. is a Philippine logistics and trucking company. The website should feel trustworthy, operational, and professional, while the internal tools should prioritize speed, clarity, and accurate records.

## Core Operations

VNS handles trucking and cargo movement for clients. The internal system should support day-to-day fleet and staff operations such as:

- Dispatch coordination
- Truck repair requests
- GPS / iTrackCare monitoring
- Payroll preparation
- Cash advances, budgets, bali, diesel PO, and reimbursements
- Parts inventory and stock movement
- Expense tracking
- Management reporting

## Likely Users

### Owner / Management

Needs dashboards, totals, status summaries, cost visibility, and quick answers. Management views should show what needs attention first.

### Dispatch / Operations Staff

Needs fast data entry, clear trip and truck status, plate-number lookup, and simple workflows that match how work is done in Viber and Google Sheets.

### Repair / Maintenance Team

Needs clear repair requests, issue categories, status tracking, parts used, labor cost, outside shop cost, odometer, and completion notes.

### Payroll / Admin

Needs driver/helper payroll records, attendance or trip-based pay, deductions, advances, reimbursements, and exportable summaries.

### Inventory Staff

Needs stock-in, stock-out, low-stock alerts, plate-number assignment, item movement history, supplier details, and cost tracking.

## Business Priorities

- Know which trucks are working, under repair, or idle.
- Reduce lost repair and parts records.
- Track cash requests and diesel PO cleanly.
- Make payroll and deductions easier to review.
- Improve management visibility without forcing staff into complex software.
- Keep tools simple enough for everyday use.

## Existing Workflow Reality

VNS operations may involve Viber messages, phone calls, handwritten notes, Google Sheets, and staff memory. The software should help convert messy operational data into structured records without making the staff workflow painful.

## Design Tone

Public website:

- Premium logistics brand
- Red, black, white, and clean industrial visuals
- Confident but not flashy

Internal tools:

- Dense but readable
- Clear tables and forms
- Strong status colors
- Fast scanning for plate numbers, dates, amounts, and pending actions

## Sensitive Data

Treat the following as private:

- Driver/helper names
- GCash numbers and phone numbers
- Payroll amounts
- Cash advances and deductions
- Client cargo details
- Truck location and GPS status
- Repair costs and supplier costs
- Internal notes and remarks

Use fake examples in code, tests, and documentation.
